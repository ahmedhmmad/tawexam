Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

Welcome to TawExam (توجيهي). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Login credentials: Seat number and password (encrypted)
  • Personal information: Full name, branch/major, school name
  • Exam data: Submitted answers, scores, time taken, submission dates
  • Session data: Authentication tokens to secure your account

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not access the camera or microphone
  • We do not access contacts
  • We do not use advertising identifiers
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not display advertisements

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and log you in
  • To deliver exams and display results
  • To sync your data between device and server
  • To improve the user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted storage on your device
  • All communication with our server uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored on secure servers
  • Some data is stored locally on your device to support offline functionality

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party. Your data is used exclusively to provide the application's services.

6. Permissions Used

  • Internet (INTERNET): To connect to the server, download exams, and submit answers
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): To detect connectivity status and support offline mode

7. Your Rights

  • You can request to view your stored data
  • You can request correction of your data
  • You can request deletion of your account and data

8. Children

Our app is designed for secondary school (Tawjihi) students. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the application.
