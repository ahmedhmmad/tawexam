Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

We respect your right to delete your personal data from TawExam (توجيهي). You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, send an email to:

admin@megaserv.xyz

Please include the following information in your request:

  1. Your seat number
  2. Your full name
  3. An explicit request for data deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon approval of your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information (name, seat number, encrypted password)
  • Exam history and answers
  • Scores and results
  • All data associated with your account

Locally Stored Data

In addition to deleting data from our servers, you can delete locally stored data on your device by:

  • Uninstalling the application from your device
  • Or clearing the app data from your device settings

Processing Time

We will process your request within a maximum of 30 days from the date of receiving your request.

Warning: Data deletion is a permanent and irreversible action. You will permanently lose all exam records and results.

Contact Us

For any questions about data deletion:

Email: admin@megaserv.xyz
